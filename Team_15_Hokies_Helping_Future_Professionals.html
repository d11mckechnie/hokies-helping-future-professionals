About the Project

Our team is partnering with Blacksburg High School's DECA chapter to enhance their fundraising initiatives through improved sales strategies and promotional support. BHS DECA currently sells chocolate chip cookies after school as a fundraiser, with sessions occurring a few days each month and lasting approximately 15 minutes.

With current average earnings of approximately $65 per session, this project focuses on doubling revenue through strategic improvements in sales promotion, team confidence building, and establishing sustainable fundraising methods. We aim to give back to the local high school community while teaching valuable business concepts we've learned at Virginia Tech.

Key Objectives

  • Improve sales strategies and promotional tactics
  • Increase confidence of the DECA sales team through mentorship and support
  • Create lasting fundraising methods for the DECA sales and marketing team
  • Establish and maintain a healthy partnership with our sponsor organization
  • Double current profits and increase revenue per session
